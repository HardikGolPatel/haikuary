Welcome back

haikuary began in 2011, and some things have changed since then.

Most people no longer write their haiku on the web, so haikuary has moved into a beautiful new app. It’s still a personal project, but no longer invitation-only; ready to grow as I had always hoped.

Your original haiku are preserved. Just download the app, sign in with your original email, reset your password, and your haiku diary will appear with all your haiku and dates intact.

Images for your old haiku will be random, but you can always change them. Any new haiku you write can include your own photographs, or you can choose from a series of calming images to match your theme.

Thank you for being one of the original members and here’s to many more years of haikuary.
